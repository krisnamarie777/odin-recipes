Cranberry Feta Pinwheels with Jalapeno

Makes 24 Pinwheels

Creamy, Tangy, Sweet and with some heat. I can't wait to make these!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 5 ounces feta chesse, crumbled
  • 1 cup dries cranberries, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup minced jalapenos, seeds and ribs removed, or to taste
  • !/3 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 2 (10–inch) spinach flour wraps or tortillas

Directions

  1. Beat cream cheese and feta together until smooth. Stir in cranberries, jalapenos, walnuts, and thyme.
  2. Spread half the filling over tortilla, leaving a 1/2 border around the edge. Roll up tightly in plastic wrap. Repeat with remaining tortilla and filling. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. To serve, trim the ends of the tortilla rolls, slice each roll into 1/2 inch pinwheels, and serve.
