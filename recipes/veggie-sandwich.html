Veggie Sandwich

Makes 1 Sandwich

Another versatile option which is chop full of veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 bagel, sliced in half
  • 1 tablespoon coarse ground mustard
  • 1 leaf romaine lettuce
  • 2 rings green bell pepper, red would also be good here
  • 4 slices cucumber
  • 2 slices tomato
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup alfalfa sprouts

Directions

  1. Spread mustard on Bagel
  2. Layer lettuce, green pepper, cucumber, and, tomato on one half.
  3. Season tomato with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with onion and alfalfa sprouts.
  5. Cover with other bagel half
