Breakfast Quesadilla

A simple and easy quesadilla.

This recipe is great for breakfast but can easily be made for lunch or other meals too.

This quesadilla can easily be customized with other similar veggies or meat.

Makes 2 servings of 3 wedges each.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil –or– other neutral medium smoke-point oil
    • 4 slices ham, diced small
  • 1/4 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • salt and black pepper tp taste
  • 2 (0 inch) flour tortillas
  • 6 slices mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat
    2. Once pan is heated, add ham and red bell pepper cook and stir until softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add eggs, cook stirring gently, until scrambled and set, 3-5 minutes. Transfer contents to bowl.
  3. Wipe out skillet and place 1 tortilla on bottom. Place 3 slices mozzarella cheese on top. Top with egg mixture and remaining 3 slices of cheese. cover with second tortilla
  4. Cook until golden, about 1 minute.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until second side is golden, for about an additional minute.
  6. Cut tortilla stack into half and then cut each half into 3rds.
  7. Enjoy
